WORK EXPERIENCES

Quality Control (QC)- Eng Joo Precious Plastic

(2021-2022)

Before pursuing my degree, I had experience working as a Quality Control (QC) at Engjoo Precious Plastics company. In business, QC plays an important role in ensuring that the quality of the company's products is maintained or increased in an effort to reach perfection. QC is in charge of training employees, defining benchmarks for product quality, and testing the product to statistically check for major differences in final product specifications. Quality Control is used to ensure that defective or damaged products do not reach customers. I work at the company to gain new experiences and broaden my knowledge so that after I finish my degree, I will be ready to enter the world of work.
